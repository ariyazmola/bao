About Dr. Bao Tran

Dr. Bao Tran, Holistic Dentist

Driven by the commitment to address root causes and better understand oral health concerns, Dr. Bao Tran and her team have embraced an approach to dentistry that focuses not just on oral health, but on whole-body wellness. This philosophy is known as "holistic dentistry." By considering patients' physical and emotional wellbeing, holistic dentistry supports essential health foundations—proper breathing, optimal nutrition, and quality sleep—all of which contribute to lasting oral health.

A dental surgeon and certified myofunctional therapist, Dr. Bao Tran earned her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree in 2001 from the University of Montreal. In the early 2010s, she began advanced training in myotherapy through courses with leading institutions, including Myofunctional Research Co. (creator of the Myobrace® system).

As a holistic dentist, Dr. Tran has developed a practice that empowers patients through education—helping them understand their unique health needs to achieve sustainable oral wellness.
